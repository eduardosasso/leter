What is Leter?

Leter is a web app that helps people create and publish a personal website.

We intentionally designed Leter to be content oriented with a selected set of features so you don't feel overwhelmed with too many options.

You focus on the writing and we'll give you just enough control to make your site look clean and aesthetically pleasing.

We are currently in stealth mode but you can check some sites already running on Leter here and here.

Leter costs $25/year but there's also a free version with some limitations.

If you want in send a shout to hello@leter.co and we’ll get back to you.

Take it easy!
